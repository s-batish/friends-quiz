The One with the Ultimate Friends Quiz

How to play

Do you think you’re the Ultimate Friends Quiz Champion? Or will you struggle like Rachel making a trifle?

Take the Ultimate Friends Quiz to find out!

The rules are simple:

  • Once you have returned to the Home page, click Play
  • You will be faced with a series of 10 questions and have 20 seconds to answer them
  • You will be awarded 10 points for each correct question and any time remaining on your questions will be added to your score – so try to answer the questions as quickly as you can

Good luck and enjoy!
